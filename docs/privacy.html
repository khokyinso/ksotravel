Privacy Policy

KSO Travel Automation — Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

KSO Travel Automation collects publicly available travel trend data from platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, and Google Trends for content creation purposes. We do not collect personal data from end users beyond what is provided through platform APIs (e.g., usernames of users who comment trigger phrases on our posts).

2. How We Use Information

Data collected is used solely for generating travel content, optimizing affiliate link performance, and sending automated direct messages to users who opt in by commenting trigger phrases on our videos. No data is sold to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok API, Meta Graph API, Supabase, and affiliate platforms (Klook, GetYourGuide, Viator, Booking.com). Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handled on their platforms.

4. Data Storage

Operational data (trend signals, content briefs, publishing logs) is stored securely on Supabase. No sensitive personal user data is stored beyond platform-provided usernames necessary for DM delivery.

5. User Rights

Users who interact with our content via comments or DMs can request data deletion by contacting us directly. We will remove any stored interaction data within 30 days of request.
