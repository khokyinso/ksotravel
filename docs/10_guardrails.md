# 10 — Content Guardrails

---

## KSO Brand Voice

| Principle | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Confident** | You've been there, you know this firsthand | "I spent 7 months in Asia so you don't have to guess" |
| **Specific** | Real numbers, real names, real places | "$90 cheaper" not "much cheaper" |
| **Urgent** | FOMO through information, not pressure | "Golden Week starts in 3 weeks" not "book now!" |
| **Concise** | Every word earns its place | 8 words per line max |
| **First-person** | Personal experience outperforms generic tips | "I saved $90" > "you can save $90" |
| **Respectful** | Culturally aware, especially sensitive destinations | See per-destination rules below |

---

## Universal Rules — All 12 Channels

### Never post:
- Prices more than 30 days out of date
- Duplicate topic within 60 days on same channel
- More than 3 same-category videos per channel per day
- Visa/entry info without web verification on publication day
- Safety claims without a verified source

### Always include:
- Real price in USD
- Real product or place name
- Affiliate promo code KSOTRAVEL
- Comment trigger phrase CTA
- Follow CTA before affiliate CTA

### Hook Blacklist (never use these openers):

```
❌ "If you are traveling to X..."
❌ "Here are some tips for..."
❌ "In this video I will show you..."
❌ "Today I want to talk about..."
❌ "Hey guys! Today we're going to..."
❌ "I just wanted to share..."
❌ "So I recently went to..."
```

### Proven Hook Patterns (use these):

```
✅ "Stop wasting money on [X] in [destination]"
✅ "Most tourists make this mistake in [destination]"
✅ "Don't visit [destination] without knowing this"
✅ "I saved $X by doing this instead"
✅ "Never book [X] before checking [Y]"
✅ "[Destination] locals never tell tourists this"
✅ "Avoid [destination] during [X] — here's why"
✅ "[Price] is too much for [X] in [destination]"
```

### 🇵🇱 Poland — STRICT RULES

**⚠️ AUSCHWITZ CONTENT:**
The following rules apply to ANY content mentioning Auschwitz-Birkenau:

**NEVER use these hooks:**
```
❌ "You HAVE to visit Auschwitz"
❌ "Auschwitz is a must-see in Poland"
❌ "My Auschwitz experience"
❌ "Auschwitz surprised me"
❌ "What nobody tells you about Auschwitz"
```

**ALWAYS frame Auschwitz content as:**
- Practical visitor information (booking, what to expect)
- Educational context
- Respectful historical acknowledgment

**Approved Auschwitz hook patterns:**
```
✅ "Visiting Auschwitz — what you need to know before you go"
✅ "How to book Auschwitz — it requires advance reservation"
✅ "Auschwitz visitor guide — practical information"
```

**Other Poland rules:**
- No sensationalist framing of WWII historical content
- Budget/value content is the strongest angle for Poland — lean into it
- Polish food content performs well — use this as a positive hook

### 🇨🇳 China — STRICT RULES

**⚠️ POLITICAL CONTENT — ZERO TOLERANCE:**
- Never mention: Tiananmen Square protests or 1989 events in any context
- Never mention: Taiwan political status
- Never mention: Xinjiang/Tibet political situations
- Never mention: Government censorship framed as criticism
- Never mention: "Communist" as a framing device
- Never compare Chinese government to other governments

**If any of the above topics appear in comments → no reply, flag to logs**

**⚠️ VPN CONTENT — STRICT FRAMING:**
- ✅ ALLOWED: "You'll need a VPN for Google Maps and Instagram while in China"
- ✅ ALLOWED: "Download a VPN before you arrive — apps like ExpressVPN work in China"
- ✅ ALLOWED: "Set up your VPN before landing — can't download them inside China"
- ❌ NEVER: Frame VPN as circumventing government censorship
- ❌ NEVER: Political commentary about internet restrictions
- ❌ NEVER: Recommend VPNs for anything other than practical tourist needs

**⚠️ VISA CONTENT — MANDATORY VERIFICATION:**
China visa policy changes frequently. Before ANY video mentioning:
- Visa-free status for any nationality
- E-visa availability
- Visa on arrival
- Port of entry policies

→ Agent 6 MUST web-verify against official Chinese embassy website for the
relevant nationality before approving the script.

**⚠️ WECHAT/ALIPAY:**
- Foreigner access to WeChat Pay and Alipay has changed multiple times
- Current status (as of March 2026): foreigners CAN link international credit cards
- MUST verify current status before every video on this topic
- If policy has changed → regenerate brief immediately

**Approved China content categories:**
- Great Wall sections (which to visit, how to book, crowd avoidance) ✅
- Beijing highlights (Forbidden City, Temple of Heaven, hutongs) ✅
- Shanghai (The Bund, French Concession, food) ✅
- Chinese food guide for tourists ✅
- High-speed rail booking via Trip.com ✅
- Practical payment setup (Alipay/WeChat Pay for foreigners) ✅
- Visa process and requirements (verify on publish day) ✅
- VPN practical setup (tourist framing only) ✅
- Timing tips (Golden Week avoidance, etc.) ✅
- Translation apps and navigation tips ✅

## Affiliate Compliance

**Disclosure:** The promo code KSOTRAVEL in every video implicitly signals
an affiliate relationship — this is consistent with FTC disclosure guidelines
for short-form video where full disclosure is impractical.

**Never claim:**
- "Cheapest in the world"
- "100% guaranteed"
- "Best [X] in [destination]" (superlatives without basis)
- Specific savings amounts unless based on real current price comparison

**Price accuracy:** All prices in scripts must be within 15% of current live
prices as verified by Agent 6's audit at time of production.

## Audit Failure Protocol

When Agent 6 returns FAIL verdict:

1. Brief is killed — never published
2. Agent 3 generates a replacement brief for that slot
3. Failure reason logged to `logs/audit_log.json`
4. If same channel fails 3+ briefs in one day → Telegram alert to Kelvin

Common failure reasons:
- Price more than 15% off live price
- Affiliate URL returning 404 or product sold out
- China visa info unverified or outdated
- Duplicate topic within 60-day window
- Script exceeds line count for target length
- Blacklisted hook opener used
