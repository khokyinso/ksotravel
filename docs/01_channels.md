# 01 — Channels & Posting Strategy
> 12 destination channels | Audience-based posting times (not destination timezone)

---

## Critical Concept: Who Is the Audience?

**Your audience is NOT people living in these countries.**
Your content is in English. Your affiliate links are Western platforms.
Your followers are English-speaking tourists planning to travel there.

```
@kso.japan audience = Americans, Canadians, Australians, Brits planning a Japan trip
@kso.france audience = Americans, Canadians planning a France trip
— NOT Japanese people, NOT French people
```

**Posting times must match when your actual audience is scrolling** —
not when people in the destination country are awake.

---

| Window | EST | Why |

**Rule: All 12 channels target US-centric peak times.**
Agent 15 refines per-channel times after 30 days of real engagement data.
